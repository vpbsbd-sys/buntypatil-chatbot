How to Set Up the WhatsApp Bot
1

Get a WhatsApp Business API number

Go to business.whatsapp.com or sign up with a provider like Gupshup or WATI. You'll need a dedicated SIM that is NOT already linked to personal WhatsApp.

2

Sign up for a chatbot platform

We recommend Gupshup.io for Marathi support. Create a free account → New Bot → WhatsApp channel → connect your number.

3

Import the bot flow (use the script file)

Use the whatsapp_bot_script.txt file provided — it contains the complete Marathi + English conversation flow to paste into your bot builder.

4

Connect to Google Sheets

In your chatbot platform, add a Webhook action that calls your Google Apps Script URL (from google_apps_script.js). This saves every complaint straight to your sheet.

5

Update the Script URL in this portal

Open index.html, find SCRIPT_URL near the top of the <script> section, and replace it with your deployed Google Script URL.

6

Go live & share your number

Print the QR code on office banners, share on social media, and put it on your website. Citizens just message "नमस्कार" to start filing.

💡 Recommended Providers for Maharashtra

• Gupshup – Best Marathi language support, used by Maharashtra govt schemes. Free tier available.
• WATI – Easy drag-and-drop builder. Good for small teams. ~₹2,500/month.
• Interakt – Built-in CRM + WhatsApp tracking. ~₹999/month.
• Twilio – Full code control if you want custom features.
